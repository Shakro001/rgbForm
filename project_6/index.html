Beyond The Galaxy Fringe

Is a thrilling arcade shooter that takes you on an epic journey through the vast expanse of space. In a world coming from the most fantastic dreams, you will take on the role of a brave space pilot, standing in defense of humanity from the invasion of alien forces.

Features:

  • Explosive Gameplay

    Unlock new weapons and technologies, modify and enhance them. Create your own unique strategy to face a variety of enemy threats.

  • Intense Bosses

    Face huge and powerful bosses with unique attacks and tactics. Develop your skills and find weaknesses to overpower these colossi.

  • Multiple Spaceships

    Choose your ship from a variety of spacecraft, each with its own strengths and unique weaponry. Upgrade them to meet increasingly difficult challenges.

  • Upgrade Weapons

    Unlock new weapons and technologies, modify and enhance them. Create your own unique strategy to face a variety of enemy threats.

  • Epic Graphics

    Immerse yourself in amazingly detailed space landscapes, stunning special effects, and gorgeous ship designs. The game looks like a true work of art.

  • Multiplayer Mode

    Engage in battles with other players in exciting online battles. Test your skills in unique multiplayer modes and compete to be the best pilot in the galaxy.

Beyond the Galaxy Fringe - will give you a unique experience filled with dynamic battles, exciting gameplay and exhilarating adventures in the vast expanses of space. Get ready to take off and join the fight for the future of mankind!"
